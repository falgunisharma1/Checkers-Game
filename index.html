Checkers Mania

Game Rules:


  • Two-player game: players can only move their checkers diagonally forward in dark-colored boxes toward the opponent's direction.
  • If a piece/checker reaches the last row ahead, it becomes a king checker, able to move diagonally forward and backward within dark-colored boxes.
  • Jump the checker by moving your piece straight over theirs and landing on the empty dark space.
  • Consecutive jumps are allowed, called "double jumps," if a landing after one jump enables another, within a single turn.
  • Winner will be the player which has the highest points when the game ends.
